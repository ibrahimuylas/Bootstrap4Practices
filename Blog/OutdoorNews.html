Bismarck angler breaks long-standing North Dakota walleye record

With its two boat ramps and ample parking on a lazy stretch of the Missouri River in Bismarck, Fox Island Park is an ideal place to drop a boat. And a line. With a fish-cleaning station and plenty of shore-fishing options, it’s a legitimate fishing location, regarded as one of the better stretches for fishing walleyes on the Missouri in Bismarck. After Friday, May 18, its reputation as a walleye fishing hotspot – and now, too, its place in North Dakota fishing history – is firmly secured.
